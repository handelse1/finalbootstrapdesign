About Us

At J-Petal, we specialize in handcrafted Japanese crepes, sweet drinks, colorful poké bowls, and Thai-inspired rolled ice cream.

Our crepes are made with 100% gluten-free batter, prepared fresh with organic flour, organic sugar, and high-quality ingredients. Whether you're in the mood for a light, flavorful drink or a custom poké bowl filled with fresh toppings, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

For a sweet finish, our Thai-style ice cream rolls are made right before your eyes, blending natural fruits, vegetables, and other fresh ingredients into a treat that's as fun to watch as it is to eat.

J-Petal is a place where simple ingredients come together to create fresh flavors you'll want to come back for.

Our Menu
